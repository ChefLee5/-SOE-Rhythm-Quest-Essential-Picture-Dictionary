## Curation Principles

1. **Character Integration**: Every scene centers around the Land's hero duo.
2. **Sound Before Symbol**: Phonetic guides are included for every word.
3. **Contextualized Sentences**: Every word is used in a narrative sentence relevant to the SOE world.
4. **Bilingual Support**: Blank space for users to write translations, fostering home language equity.
5. **Character Tips**: Each sub-scene concludes with a character's pedagogical tip.
6. **ASL Inclusion**: Back matter includes full ASL alphabet (A-Z, 0-10) and 100 essential signs across 4 categories.
